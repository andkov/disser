Diamond Cut, Size, and Price

Diamonds with an ideal cut have a lower median price.

## Warning: package 'ggplot2' was built under R version 2.15.3

One explanation for this unexpected finding is that ideal cut diamonds also tend to be smaller.
The mean is \( E(carat | cut = Ideal) = 0.7028 \), and size is related to price.
